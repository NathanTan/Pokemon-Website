Venusaur


Fun Fact: Venusaur is the first pokemon in the pokedex, because he is the best.

(of course only Venusaurs beginning stages, Bulbasaur and Ivysaur, come before him in the pokedex)

When Brock was high upon the mountain tops, Arceus revealed the truths of the earth.
First thing he mentioned was a bad ass dinosaur.
You'll never find another creature so vicious;
with Venusaur success always is propitious

Step back fool! Don't be playing around with Venusaur or he'll mess you up. Dinosaurs have been around for over 231.4 million years. How long did it take you to graduate high school? Maybe you should go back so you can learn not to mess with dinosaurs. If you cross Venusaur you better get your ass ready for a frenzy plant ass whooping. And unless you're a genetic combination of Arnold Swartzinagger and the Hulk, then there's absolutely no way you are making any ground against the mighty Venusaur.

Tactically, Venusaur is a gem. With the chlorophyll ability, you can sweep your opponent with a Sunny Day/Solar Beam combination. If you're into the whole mega-evolution thing then you can tank Charziards flamethower for days with your thick fat ability.

Or if you're trying to be a bitch and run a stall set you can load up on the leech seed and just use synthesis and toxic stall your opponent out.

Now I may have a biased view, but simply because Bulbasuar was my first pokemon ever. So if you can't appreciate the wonders that Venusaur holds then I've no patients for your lousy love for pokemon.
