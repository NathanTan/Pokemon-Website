More space for Blastoise that way.

So here it is. the obligatory Blastoise page. Blastoise isn't all that bad. I mean, he's got the cannons on his back right?

Those are pretty cool. But, of course, not as cool as Venusaur or Charizard. If you think about it, Blastoise is the only 1st Gen starter that did not receive dual typing. That would be my guess as to why there was no blue remake but a Leaf Green 3rd Gen remake.

It is important to remember what type we are dealing with. While Blastoise does have awesome cannons and a bulky look with its shell, Blastoise is a water type. I mean no intentional discrimination with this statement. Water is a cool type, but water type starters suffer greatly from being forced into the most over-populated type. Water type pokemon make up roughly 16% of all pokemon. With so many water types there was bound to be some overlap. The most obvious example of this would be Blastoise and Feraligatr; their base stat total is the same, their type is the same, their competitive smogon rank is the same, and the are back to back in the generation line up. Speaking of competitive rank, 4 out of 6 of the water starters are Under-used. We can see the compensation for the lack of formidable water starters with the appearance of Greninja.

Greninja is a monster. With Greninja's breakneck speed and killer speacial attack topped of with its hidden ability Protean, Greninja was built to put Blaziken in its place.
